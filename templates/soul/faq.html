FAQs

How do I play a match?

Create or join an open match Players can see each others PlayStation network / Xbox live names on game details in Gaming Frog. Online Friendlies Select “New Friendly Season” Select “Name of Friend” At next screen, select “Invite” Next screen enables players to select LEAGUE and CLUB Both players must play as they agreed or the game is null and void. After selecting your club, press “A” or “X” to “Ready!” your selection. Wait for your opponent to “Ready!” their selection as well. When both players are “Ready!” then play the match!

How do I report results?

Once a match is finished players can enter results in the match. Both users have to agree to results. Once results are agreed, funds will be debited to the winners account. In the case that you or your opponent don’t agree to a result a dispute will be created.

What is the maximum amount of money I can play in single matches?

The maximum amount of money you can play in single matches is 1000rs.
